Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasoning
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Layer the lasagna.
  4. Cover with foil and bake.
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
